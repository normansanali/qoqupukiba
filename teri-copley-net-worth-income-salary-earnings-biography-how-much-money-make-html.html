    Teri Copley (born May 10, 1961) is a model and actress from the United States. She is most known for her performances in the American television series We Got it Made and the film I Had Three Wives, both of which she co-starred in. Copley had an appearance on the dating game show Dating Game in 1979, and she dated Joe Banashek at the time. 

    She also had roles in the television movies I Married a Centerfold (1984) and Brain Donors (1991), both of which she appeared in. She was on the cover of Playboy magazine’s November 1990 edition, when she posed naked. Copley became a born-again Christian in the 1990s, and she gradually transitioned out of the spotlight of famous Hollywood. As of December 2005, she was still a resident of the state of California.
