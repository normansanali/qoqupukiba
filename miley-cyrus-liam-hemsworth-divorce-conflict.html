    Whether it be an outrageous outfit or a new single, singer-songwriter Miley Cyrus has always kept her fans on their toes. While Miley owes most of her fanbase to her character Miley Ray Stewart in the Disney Channel Television series Hannah Montana which gained worldwide commercial success, she has also acquired recognition for her work as a musician.

    Cyrus' relationship with Hemsworth was never a secret affair, nor was their split. Miley has always been candid about her life events, whether her sexuality or her divorce from ex-beau Liam. Since she believes in being as open with her fans as she can, a gesture that her admirers often reciprocate by praising her openness.

    Updated on February 2nd, 2022 by Abigail Thomas: Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth, despite their long history, were only married for a very short time. They originally dated when they were teenagers after meeting on set of The Last Song in 2009. The first of many breakups occurred in 2010, but they got back together and became engaged in 2012. They officially called off the engagement in late 2013 after previously postponing the wedding. The celebrity couple then secretly got married in 2018, but were divorcing within a year. Now, years later, Cyrus has released a song called "Flowers" in preparation for her upcoming album. The song seems to discuss her previous relationship with Hemsworth, and fans are freaking out over the details and secrets in the song.

    Liam And Miley Met As Teenagers

    While people know that Miley Cyrus met Liam Hemsworth on the set of the 2009 movie The Last Song, they are unaware that it was not the duo's first encounter with each other, as they had met when Hemsworth was auditioning for the movie.

    Related: Miley Cyrus And Other Musicians Who Gave Fans The Best Songs About Moving On

    During an appearance on The Ellen DeGeneres show in 2010, the 7 Things singer recalled her first interaction with her then-boyfriend Liam.

    "I met him and he opened the door for me and I was like, 'I have been in LA for three years and I don't think any guy has actually opened the door for me,'" the songstress told Ellen.

    "[Liam has] become my best friend in the whole wide world," Cyrus had told Parade in an interview in 2010. "I love him. He really respects me for who I am."

    In another interview with the same magazine, she had claimed that Liam was 'her first serious boyfriend' despite her previous dating history with Nick Jonas, whom she dissed in one of her recent concerts, and Justin Gaston.

    Liam proposed to the Midnight Sky singer with a giant 3.5-carat Neil Lane engagement ring in 2012. The couple had posed for a silly Christmas picture in the same year with Miley's brother Trace Cyrus and mom Tish Cyrus. The singer has had a problematic relationship with her mother for some time, but fans hoped the marriage would be just what Miley needed.

    Miley and Liam ended up postponing their wedding and ultimately canceling it in late 2013. Though they broke up, fans always hoped they would find their way back to each other. Though they did, the celebrities experienced a lot more drama down the road.

    Miley Said There Was 'Too Much Conflict'

    Miley and Liam privately got married in December 2018, but sadly, things didn't seem to work out for the twosome as they parted ways within a year of tying the knot. The news came as a shock to many when their split was announced.

    Numerous sources confirmed their separation to Us Weekly in August 2019. "Liam and Miley have agreed to separate at this time," a rep for the singer said in a statement.

    Related: Miley Cyrus May Be Dragging Liam In Her New Music, But Fans Spotted More Shady Details

    The Isn't It Romantic star filed for divorce from Cyrus on August 19, 2019, citing "irreconcilable differences." On August 22, 2019, the Slide Away singer tweeted denying infidelity to her husband. Their divorce was finalized in January 2020.

    There could be numerous reasons for the couple to divorce, none of which we can be sure about but the Midnight Sky singer opened up on the matter while promoting her seventh studio album, Plastic Hearts, on an episode of The Howard Stern Show in December 2020.

    In the interview mentioned above, Miley insisted that her battles with sobriety at the time had nothing to do with her divorce and that her relationship ended only because "there was too much conflict."

    While many people, peers and fans alike, were heartbroken by the news of the couple's divorce, Liam Hemsworth's mother was one of the people who were happy about her son's decision, according to a close source to The Hunger Games star.

    "His mom is feeling so relieved that Liam made the decision to end the relationship as his entire family, but her especially always struggled to approve Miley," the source had told Hollywood Life.

    This was because Liam's family has always wanted "to see him with someone much more traditional."

    Hemsworth And Cyrus Moved On Swiftly

    To avoid being a part of the drama, shortly after announcing his divorce Liam Hemsworth, accompanied by his elder brother Chris Hemsworth, went on a long vacation to Australia, where they were spotted on Richard Branson's Makeshift Island.

    Following the split from Miley, a source told Us Weekly that Hemsworth was "open to meeting people." In October 2019, Liam was seen holding hands with an Australian actress named Maddison Brown.

    However, in December 2019, sparks flew between the Australian actor and English actress Gabrielle Brooks. Hemsworth's relationship with Brooks is going strong to date.

    Miley Cyrus was seen kissing Katelyn Carter during a trip to Italy in August 2019, when her split with husband Liam was announced. Carter and Cyrus split after a month of PDA in the U.S.

    Related: Did Miley Cyrus And Liam Hemsworth Have A Prenup For A Specific Reason?

    In October 2019, the We Can't Stop singer started dating Cody Simpson, whom she broke up with in August 2020. Miley was spotted spending quality time with Maxx Morando on New Year's Eve in 2021. Miley and Maxx have been dating since then.

    Miley Cyrus Releases 'Flowers': Liam Hemsworth Diss Track

    In January 2023, Miley Cyrus opened up more to fans about her former relationship and marriage to Liam Hemsowrth. She released the single "Flowers," which seems to be a discussion of their relationship and her positive mental state going forward.

    Fans have been diving deep into the hidden messages within the song. There are many references to Liam throughout the song, and some fans even think Miley is clueing in the world on Liam's cheating. The song also seems to be a female remake of Bruno Mars's "When I Was Your Man," which some appreciate but others are not thrilled about the rewrite.
