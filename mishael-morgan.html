    How did Mishael Morgan start her Professional Career?

    Mishael Morgan’s professional career began with a recurrent role in the Canadian adolescent drama series The Best Years from 2008 to 2009, followed by a permanent role in the YTV comedy Family Biz.

    In 2012, she had a recurring part in the CBC Television comedy-drama Republic of Doyle, and she also had small roles in the films Casino Jack (2010) and Total Recall (2012).

    She later featured in Supernatural and The Listener as a guest performer. Furthermore, in 2014, this actress was featured in The CW’s summer comedic series Backpackers. In May 2013, Morgan was cast as Hilary Curtis on The Young and the Restless, a CBS daytime serial drama.

    She stated that she will be departing the show on June 11, 2018. Morgan’s representative subsequently confirmed that she chose to depart after unsuccessful salary negotiations.

    She made her final appearance as Hilary on the serial on July 27, 2018, when the character perished from life-threatening injuries suffered in a car crash. She revealed her return to the show on September 19, 2019, the same day she starred as Amanda Sinclair.

    On June 24, 2022, she became the first black woman to receive the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series for her depiction of Amanda Sinclair.
