    Biography

    Sam Cox was born in Kent, England on March 8, 1994. Better known as Mr. Doodle, he is an artist and illustrator from London who is known for his unique style of detailed doodles. His work appears on a variety of mediums ranging from clothes to walls to anything else he gets his hands on and they are often in black and white, featuring a wide range of characters, concepts, and linework. He shares his illustration work with his over 1.6 million Instagram followers. His intricate style of drawing along with his cartoon inspired characters reflect the work of pop art sensationalist Keith Haring. He first began sharing images of his art to Instagram in May of 2013.
