    BIOGRAPHY
    Terence Crawford is an American professional boxer, born on September 28, 1987, in Omaha, Nebraska. He began boxing at the age of seven, achieving a record of 58 wins in 70 fights and by 2006 won the lightweight silver medal at the National Golden Gloves and a lightweight bronze medal at the championships. of the United States. During his amateur days he also defeated subsequent professional world champions Miguel García and Danny García. As a professional, he won his first fight in March 2008 and little by little he rose in his career, in various weights, obtaining the WBO and WBC titles and named number 1 by Ring Magazine, in 2016, in the light welterweight category. .
