    Is Laird Hamilton Related To Bethany Hamilton? Do the surfers know each other? Tune in to find out.

    Laird Hamilton is a professional big-wave surfer who co-invented tow-in surfing. His talents do not just lie in surfing but also branch up to modeling.

    After leaving his high school in eleventh grade, Laird pursued a modeling career. Likewise, he was discovered by a photographer from the Italian Men’s Vogue magazine L’Uomo Vogue on a beach in Kaua’i, which helped him get a modeling contract.

    Hamilton has also appeared in different movies like North Shore, Moving target, and many more.

    Is Laird Hamilton Related To Bethany Hamilton?

    No, Laird Hamilton is not related to Bethany Hamilton. Both of them grew up in Kauai but are not related to each other. Bethany, herself has mentioned this on her website.

    Since both the surfers share the same last name, people assume that they are related.

    Moreover, Laird and Bethany are good friends but are not related.

    Meet Laird Hamilton Wife Gabrielle Reece

    Laird Hamilton is happily married to his wife, Gabrielle Reece. As a fact, both Gabrielle and Laird stand tall with a stature of 6 feet and 3 inches.

    Talking about Gabrielle, she is a professional volleyball player, model, and actress. She earned a volleyball scholarship to Florida State University, where she studied in communications.

    Reece also established two school volleyball records, one for solo blocks (240) and the other for total blocks (747), which she still holds. She moved to New York City in 1989 to pursue a parallel career as a sports fashion model while continuing her pro volleyball career.

    In 1997, Florida State University inducted Reece into the university’s Athletic Hall of Fame.

     Laird Hamilton Children and Family Names

    Likewise, Laird and Gabrielle have two daughters together, Reece Viola Hamilton and Brody Jo Hamilton.

    The Hamilton family shares their time between Malibu, California, and Kauai, Hawaii.
