    Sound the sirens, light up the sparklers and break out the streamers, because something huge just happened in the fashion world. Revolve launched its brand new venture, superdown! All we want to do is celebrate, but first and foremost, we need to shop.

    All at an affordable price point, superdown is all about providing luxury style that’s on-trend and worthy of an OOTD hashtag. There are glamorous, celeb style-inspired pieces, streetwear stunners and shoes and accessories to perfect every outfit. To make a totally obvious pun, we are “super down” for all of it. But with so many pieces, we wanted to help get everyone started on filling up that cart. We picked out five of our favorite pieces right now, so check them out below and save superdown to that bookmarks bar . . . now!

    Maddie Cropped Wrap Sweatshirt

    We loved this sweatshirt so much upon laying eyes on it that we didn’t even notice the sale price at first! But hey, we’ll take it. This cropped sweatshirt is a Dusty Blue shade that fits seamlessly into all seasons. It has a surplice wrap hem in front and a straight hem in the back, both with a ribbed trim. Other accents include ruched detailing at the sides, as well as a diagonal seam up on the chest, mimicking direction of the hem. It also has dropped shoulders, which exude a carefree nonchalance. This sweatshirt is nothing like the kind we’d find on a football field; it’s honestly nicer than some of our best blouses!

    See it: Get the Maddie Cropped Wrap Sweatshirt (originally $58) for just $41 at superdown!

    Mae Ruffle Wrap Maxi Dress

    Fancy occasion coming up? Going glamping? Just in the mood to wear something nice? This Mae dress will fit the occasion. It’s constructed of a lightly crinkled chiffon fabric, which is a gorgeous springtime pink. It’s like a floral without the actual flower pattern. It’s lined inside, so no need to worry about adding a slip underneath! It features a zipper at the back of the skirt, as well as a tie closure across the shoulders, which is a delightfully delicate detail, as the loops and loose ends float down between our shoulder blades. It has short, fluttering butterfly sleeves and a slit at the skirt so we can let our heels show, too.

    See it: Get the Mae Ruffle Wrap Maxi Dress for just $84 at superdown!

    Drew Cropped Denim Jacket

    For a perfectly executed blend of modern-day edgy and vintage-chic, look no further than the Drew jacket. Its washed black color, raw hem and distressed collar scream grungy excellence, while the cropped fit and 100% cotton material let it be known that we know just what we’re doing in all aspects of life. This jacket has a front button closure as well as two front flap pockets, featuring the same smooth, silvery buttons. We’ll be wearing this over literally every outfit we own, including the Mae dress, thank you!

    See it: Get the Drew Cropped Denim Jacket for just $74 at superdown!

    Knox Heel

    These nude heels will go with seriously everything. They’re made with a neutral beige, cowhide leather and feature two thick, clear straps. There’s no possibility of color clashing when we’re working with clear! We also love the straps because they remind us vaguely of ‘90s jelly shoes, but with a futuristic and upscale vibe. The chunky heel is 3.5 inches high, and the shoe slips right on for a romantic date, a night of partying or a day of strutting down the office hallway!

    See it: Get the Knox Heel for just $110 at superdown!

    By the Way. Demi Skinny Jeans

    These light-wash blue jeans are the ultimate outfit enhancer. Usually we see our jeans as a base for our outfit, but we’re using these to up our T-shirt or blouse game with ease. They’re cropped above the ankle and strategically distressed near the leg opening for a fashionable flair. They feature subtle fading, as well as whiskering at the hips, and have a classic button fly. They also have front and back pockets. Pair these skinny jeans with the Knox heel or slip on a white canvas sneaker and we’ve got two totally different looks. Make sure to tuck a cute tee into that high-rise waist! And act quickly, because these jeans are on sale!

    See it: Get the By the Way. Demi Skinny Jeans (originally $78) for just $64 at superdown!
