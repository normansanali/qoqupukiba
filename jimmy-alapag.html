    Biography

    Jimmy Alapag was born in California on December 30, 1977. Decorated Filipino point guard nicknamed “Mighty Mouse” who won six PBA titles with the Talk ‘N Text Tropang Texters, with whom he played from 2003 to 2015. He was an eleven-time All-Star, led the league in assists six times and was voted the 2011 PBA MVP. He was teammates with Gabe Norwood on the Philippines squad at the Asian Games and FIBA Basketball World Cup in 2014. He grew up in San Bernardino, California and played college basketball at California State University, San Bernardino. He was selected by the Texters as the 10th overall pick in the 2003 PBA Draft.
