    Here is the list of 40 Richest Rappers in the world, with Kaye West on the top with $6.6 Billion, followed by Jay-Z with $1.3 billion.

    Whenever you hear the word “RAP,” what comes to your mind? If the answer is Rappers, then you are at the right place.

    It is okay not to know about the rappers and still enjoy the songs. So, for those who are curious about rappers, here is the list of the 40 richest rappers in the world as of December, 2022.

    40. 50 Cents

    At the 40th number, we have the American rapper, producer, actor, and entrepreneur, Curtis James Jackson III, known as 50 Cents.

    He has a total net worth of $30 million as of December, 2022. 

    Cents is famous for writing the famous underground rap single “How To Rob.”

    His famous song, “In DaClub,” has 1 billion views on Youtube.

    Further, Cents has also written famous songs like “How We Do,” “Disco Inferno,” “Down On Me,” “Guess Who’s Back,” etc.

    Over the years, he has invested in different business deals like the Effen Vodka deal, Starz Deal, Vitamin Water deal.

    39. J Prince

    At the 39th number, we have the American rapper, Businessman, James Prince know as “J Prince,” who has a total net worth of $45 million as of December, 2022.

    Likewise, Prince is the Founder and CEO of J Lab Productions and a brand ambassador for Range Water.

    J started his career as a disc jockey(DJ) for a sound system company.

    His famous album was ”Omega – A New Beginning”, and ”Renaissance”.

    Furthermore, Prince has also released the songs like “Gone,” “Thunder Roll,” “Turnaround,” etc.

    J Prince was responsible for discovering Young Money/Cash Money/Universal Records recording artist Drake.

    38. Darryl McDaniels

    At the 38th number, we have the American rapper, Darryl McDaniels, know as “DMC,” who has a total net worth of $45 million as of December, 2022.

    In 1984, DMC and his two friends, Russell Simmons and Rick Rubin, released a very successful album in the hip-hop industry.

    Later, the trio became very famous after releasing their third album, “Raising Hell,” which made it to the Billboard 200 at the rank of No. 6 on and No. 1 on the Top R & R&B/Hip-Hop Albums.

    “Christmas in Hollis,” “It’s Tricky,” “Just like me,”etc., are the famous songs of Darryl.

    37. Common

    At the 37th number, we have the  American musician, rapper, writer, and actor Common know as “Lonnie Rashid Lynn,” who has a total net worth of $45 million as of December, 2022.

    In 1992, Lynn’s debut with the album Can I Borrow a Dollar and also gained critical acclaim with his 1994 album Resurrection.

    Lynn’s famous song “Come Close to Me“ has been listed on Billboard.

    Common has received “Academy Awards,” 2 “African-American Film Critics Association,” 3 “BET Awards,” etc.

    36. T.I

    At the 36th number, we have the  American musician, rapper, songwriter, record producer, entrepreneur, and actor Clifford Joseph Harris Jr, know as “T.I,” who has a total net worth of $50 million as of December, 2022.

    T.I has 10 Billboards hit, which are:
