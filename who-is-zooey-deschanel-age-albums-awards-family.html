    Who Is Zooey Deschanel?, Age, Films, Albums, Awards, Family, Net Worth: Zooey Deschanel, officially known as Zooey Claire Deschanel is an American actress and singer.

    She developed a passion for acting and singing at a younger age, has been consistent throughout her career, and has become one of the most sought-after acts.

    Her film credit includes; Mumford, Almost Famous, The Good Girl, The New Guy, Elf, The Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy, Failure to Launch, Yes Man, and 500 Days of Summer.

    She appeared in the films; Our Idiot Brother, Manic, All the Real Girls, Winter Passing, Bridge to Terabithia, The Happening, and The Driftless Area, among others.

    Deschanel also starred in New Girl as Jessica Day, for which she received nominations for a Primetime Emmy Award and three Golden Globe Awards.

    As a singer, Deschanel in 2006 teamed up with American singer, M. Ward to form ‘She & Him’, and subsequently released their debut album, Volume One, on 18th March 2008.

    Deschanel has since then released a number of projects and has received various awards and accolades. In December 2020, she appeared in the music video for Katy Perry’s song “Not the End of the World”

    In 2021, she co-hosted the ABC television series The Celebrity Dating Game with Michael Bolton. In January 2022, she began cohosting Welcome to Our Show, a New Girl rewatch podcast with co-stars Hannah Simone and Lamorne Morris.

    Deschanel appears as Kelly in season 3 of Physical released on August 2, 2023, and Nancy in Dreamin’ Wild, which was released on August 4, 2023.

    In August 2023, Deschanel hit the headlines following her engagement to Jonathan Scott. The actress and the Property Brothers host are ready to wed, after four years of dating.

    According to reports, Scott popped the question on Sunday, August 13, 2023, during a family trip to Scotland, and Deschanel, of course, said “Yes!”

    The couple also announced the news in a joint post on their Instagram accounts on Monday, August 14 with the caption; “Forever starts now”. See the post below.

    Zooey Deschanel Age

    Zooey Deschanel celebrated her 43rd birthday in January this year (2023). She was born on January 17, 1980, in Los Angeles, California, United States.

    Zooey Deschanel Films

    Zooey Deschanel has starred in several films including; The New Guy, Your Highness, Failure To Launch, Almost Famous, Trolls, Yes Man, Our Idiot Brother, Gigantic, All The Real Girls, Rock The Kasbah, Manic, and Mumford, among others.

    Zooey Deschanel Albums

    In 2006, Zooey Deschanel teamed up with M. Ward to form She & Him, and subsequently released their debut album, Volume One, in 2008.

    They have since released six albums: Volume Two (2010), A Very She & Him Christmas (2011), Volume 3 (2013), Classics (2014), Christmas Party (2016), and Melt Away: A Tribute to Brian Wilson (2022).

    Zooey Deschanel Awards

    As of August 2023, Zooey Deschanel is a recipient of two awards; Best Actress at the 2003 Mar del Plata International Film Festival, and Choice TV Actress (Comedy) at the 2012 Teen Choice Awards. She has bagged other several nominations.

    Zooey Deschanel Family

    Zooey Deschanel was born in Los Angeles, California, United States to her parents; Mary Jo Deschanel (mother) and Caleb Deschanel (father).

    The couple have been married since 1972. Her mother is an actress best known for roles in the film; The Right Stuff and the television series Twin Peaks.

    Her father is a cinematographer and director of film and television who has been nominated for the Academy Award for Best Cinematography six times.

    Caleb Deschanel is a member of the National Film Preservation Board of the Library of Congress, representing the American Society of Cinematographers.

    The award-winning actress has an older sister named Emily Deschanel who was born on October 11, 1976, in Los Angeles, California, United States.

    Emily is also an actress. She portrayed Dr. Temperance “Bones” Brennan in the Fox crime procedural series Bones.

    Emily has been married to actor, David Hornsby since 2010. The couple are blessed with two sons.

    As of August 2023, Zooey Deschanel has been engaged, married, and divorced twice.

    In December 2008, she became engaged to Ben Gibbard, a musician and lead vocalist for Death Cab for Cutie and The Postal Service.

    They tied the knot on September 19, 2009, and on November 1, 2011, they announced their separation. Deschanel filed for divorce on December 27, 2011, which was finalized on December 12, 2012.

    Following their divorce in 2012, the actress in January 2015 confirmed her engagement to Jacob Pechenik, a film producer. They married in June 2015.

    They have two children: a daughter, Elsie born in July 2015, and a son, Charlie born in May 2017. In September 2019, Deschanel and Pechenik announced their separation, and their divorce was finalized on June 1, 2020.

    While filming in 2019, Deschanel met Jonathan Scott. After Deschanel and Pechenik announced their separation in September 2019, Deschanel started dating Scott.

    On Sunday, August 13, 2023, Deschanel and Scott announced their engagement and are ready to wed, after four years of dating.

    Jonathan Scott, officially known as Jonathan Silver Scott was born on April 28, 1978, in Vancouver, Canada to his parents; Joanne Scott and Jim Scott.

    He’s a reality television personality, construction contractor, illusionist, and television and film producer. Scott was formerly married to Kelsy Ully from 2007 until they separated in 2013.

    Zooey Deschanel Net Worth

    As of August 2023, Zooey Deschanel has an estimated net worth of about $25 million. She has earned such an amount from her career as an actress and a singer.

    Zooey Deschanel Social Media

    Zooey Deschanel owns a verified Facebook page with over 5.9 million followers, a verified Twitter account with over 5.4 million followers, and a verified Instagram account with over 7.6 million followers.
