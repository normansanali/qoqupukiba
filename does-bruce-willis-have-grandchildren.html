    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    American actor and singer Bruce Willis has officially bid farewell to the onscreen action after temporarily his battle with aphasia.

    Willis had always been keen to set out on his own acting path so he dropped out of school after his freshman year.

    He relocated to New York City to attempt to make it as a professional actor.

    He made his television debut in Moonlighting before breaking out on the big screen in films such as Die Hard, Pulp Fiction, and The Sixth Sense.

    Among his most recent films are The Expendables, Red, Moonrise Kingdom, Once Upon a Time in Venice, Acts of Violence, a remake of Death Wish and Reprisal.

    He is indeed going to be missed as he recovers this disease but what some fans have been inquiring about is whether he has grandchildren.

    We are here to answer that.

    Does Bruce Willis have grandchildren?

    Bruce Willis has five kids, three with his ex-wife Demi Moore and two with his current wife, Emma Hemingway.

    Sadly, he is not yet a grandfather but things can change in the future and wait to see him become one.
