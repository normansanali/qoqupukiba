    William Chase Daniel (born October 7, 1986) is a National Football League quarterback for the Los Angeles Chargers (NFL). He attended Missouri and was signed as an undrafted free agent by the Washington Redskins in 2009. Daniel has also played for the Kansas City Chiefs, Philadelphia Eagles, New Orleans Saints, Chicago Bears, and Detroit Lions.
