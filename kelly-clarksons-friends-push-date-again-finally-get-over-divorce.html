    Kelly Clarkson had a heartbreaking divorce from Brandon Blackstock, which dragged out for years and cost her a lot. But it’s been two years since the divorce was finalized, and it appears that the singer’s friends are encouraging her to get back into the dating game so she can officially move on.

    “Her friends and colleagues are all itching to set her up and she’s excited to go on some dates,” a source told OK! Magazine.

    Kelly’s Friends Want Her To Date, But She’s Not Ready Yet

    “Kelly’s not rushing into anything but there’s no way she’s going to sit around being single forever,” they continued. “She’s ready to smile again and get out there and have some fun.”

    It’s unclear if Kelly is currently playing the field or just thinking of getting back into the dating game. But earlier this year, the singer was quick to deny that she had a special someone in her life at the moment.

    "Do I have a boyfriend? No! And not looking. You know why? I love being single," Kelly said in December. "I have two kids, two dogs, three rabbits, a hamster, multiple jobs — there's a lot going on.”

    She also implied that she may not be fully moved on from the end of her marriage to Blackstock. “And, you know, you think you're going to spend the rest of your life with somebody, and you don't. That's hard to start over,” Kelly went on.

    Kelly and Brandon were married from 2013 to 2012 and share two kids. To settle their divorce, Kelly had to give her ex a one-time payment of $1.3 million as well as $115,000 in spousal support plus $45,000 in child support on a monthly basis.

    Kelly Is Making A Lot Of Big Life Changes Post-Divorce

    But Kelly has been making a lot of big life changes recently. Earlier this month, the singer revealed she made the decision to leave Los Angeles and relocate to New York, where she can also film her television program The Kelly Clarkson Show.

    “I’ll be real honest: I thought I was making a horrible decision,” she told USA Today. “I knew I needed a fresh start and couldn’t be in LA.” Kelly moved to Los Angeles after separating from her ex-husband.

    “I really wanted to be in Montana, but you can’t really do a show from there quite yet. So I was like, ‘The only other option would probably be New York,’” she continued. “At this point, I’m 40 years old. Mama rented something nice!”

    Moreover, it appears Kelly has been transforming her life in other ways, as fans have noticed she’s undergone a physical transformation recently, as new photos show a slimmed-down physique.

    While Kelly has received praise for the supposed weight loss, she’s also faced criticism, with some fans questioning how she’s appeared to slim down so quickly. Kelly has even faced accusations of using Ozempic, a weight loss drug that’s been criticized for being increasingly prescribed in recent months.

    For now, Kelly has refused to deny the speculation around her weight, but she has addressed it in the past and denied using medication, rather crediting her weight loss to lifestyle and dietary changes.
