    You know what they say about high school: If you don’t have your love life and professional life in order by the time your final summer before college ends, it is completely over for you. Well, at least that’s what they’re teaching at the high school Griffin (K.J. Apa, returned to his natural brunette) attends. In the trailer for Netflix’s The Last Summer, Griffin and his buddies have one last hot season in Chicago “to decide who we wanna be, and who we wanna be with.” And then it’s over! No more chances — forever! Someone should tell all these very attractive teens, played by Maia Mitchell and Jacob Latimore and Halston Sage, that there’s life after 17, because they’re putting a ton of pressure on themselves. Anyway, this breezy story of young love and dance pop and critical decisions that will shape the rest of your life starts streaming on May 3.
