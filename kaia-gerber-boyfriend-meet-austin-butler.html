    Kaia Gerber boyfriend-American model, Kaia Gerber was born on September 3rd, 2001 in Los Angeles, California in the United States of America.

    Gerber was born to the supermodel, Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber, a businessman. She shares the same parents with her elder brother, Presley who is also a model.

    She stands at a height of 1.77 m tall and weighs 54kg. Her father is a Jew with Russian ancestry. Her mother is a Christian and has German, English, French, and Danish ancestry.

    Young Versace, Versace’s junior line, hired Gerber as a model for the first time when she was ten years old. Gerber attended Malibu High School and took online courses.

    Kaia Gerber career

    At the age of 15, Gerber made her acting debut in Sister Cities as the young Carolina. At the age of 16, Gerber debuted on the Calvin Klein Collection runway in 2017 under the direction of Raf Simons. Since then, she has walked for designers including Marc Jacobs, Burberry, Alexander Wang, Coach, Prada, Chanel, Fendi, Miu Miu, Max Mara, Givenchy, Bottega Veneta, and Stella McCartney.

    Alongside her mother, she also collaborated with other fashion houses during 2018 Spring Fashion Week, including Isabel Marrant, Alberta Ferretti, Loewe, Chloé, Sacai, Michael Kors, Moncler, JW. Anderson, Longchamp, Tom Ford, Salvatore Ferragamo, Ports1961, Lanvin, Anna Sui, Moschino, Saint Laurent, Alexander McQueen, Valentino, and Versace.

    In addition to numerous international editions of Vogue, including Vogue Paris, Vogue Japan, Vogue Italia, Vogue British, Vogue India, Vogue China, and “The Book edition” for Vogue Netherlands in Winter 2016, Gerber has made editorials and appeared on the covers of fashion magazines, including The Love Magazine, i-D, Teen Vogue, and Pop Magazine, among others.

    In 2016 and 2017, Gerber was featured in campaigns for Omega and Miu Miu eyewear, respectively. Peter Lindbergh took a shot of her with her mother. She participated in advertisements for Versace, Calvin Klein, and Saint Laurent fall/winter in the spring of 2018.

    She and Sofia Mechetner were in charge of the Daisy fragrance campaign in 2018. She starred in campaigns for both Chanel and Valentino the same year, which were both photographed by Karl Lagerfeld.

    Karl Lagerfeld and Kaia Gerber worked together to produce their debut collection, the KarlxKaia line, in 2018. In 2019, Gerber appeared in the spring/summer, fall/winter, and spring campaigns for Jimmy Choo, Stella McCartney, and Fendi.

    She also made her debut as the new face of marketing campaigns and a YSL beauty brand ambassador. Gerber appeared in the spring-summer 2020 Craig McDean-shot Louis Vuitton “Twist bag” commercial campaign.

    According to Daily Front Row, a group of prominent investors bought the fashion magazine W on August 14, 2020, and Gerber was one of them. Gerber made an appearance in both the American Horror Story spinoff and the tenth season of the FX horror anthology.

    In 2021, Gerber made her debut on the cover of American Vogue, which she repeated in the September issue. She was chosen for a recurring role in the Mrs. American Pie miniseries on Apple TV+ in 2022.

    Who is Kaia Gerber dating?

    From late 2019 until February 2020, Gerber dated American comedian Pete Davidson. She started dating Australian actor Jacob Elordi in October 2020 but unfortunately, after a year, the couple split up.

    She is currently dating the actor, Austin Butler. The couple commenced their romantic relationship in December 2021.

    Who is Austin Butler?

    Austin Butler is an American actor. He began his career on television, first in roles on the Disney Channel and Nickelodeon and later on teen dramas, including recurring parts on The CW’s Life Unexpected (2010–2011) and Switched at Birth (2011–2012).

    He gained recognition for starring in The Carrie Diaries (2013–2014) and The Shannara Chronicles (2016–2017).

    What is Austin Butler’s net worth?

    Butler has a net worth estimated to be about $4 million.
