    What Up

    Today, Daz returns with a new Trippy Keez-produced single titled What Up featuring Trae Tha Truth and MJG. This too, will appear on his upcoming album Weed Money, which is expecting a April 22 release date.

    26 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Today, Daz returns with a new Trippy Keez-produced single titled “What Up” featuring Trae Tha Truth and MJG. This too, will appear on his upcoming album Weed Money, which is expecting a April 22 release date.

    Weed Money will follow up Daz's 2012 release Witit Witit, and is Daz' 15th solo studio album since he dropped his debut in 1998, Retaliation, Revenge and Get Back.

    Let us know if you're feeling the collaboration.
