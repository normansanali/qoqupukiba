    It sounds like was a bit “charmed” by Alyssa Milano.

    Two-time WWE Hall of Famer Booker T recently sat down with Tyler Treese of ComingSoon.net to discuss a variety of subjects. When asked about his memories regarding his guest spot on the television show Charmed with Scott Steiner, Booker T revealed that it was actress Alyssa Milano:

    “Alyssa Milano. That’s the memory that I have of that,” Booker T said with a long laugh. “I remember what she told me after we finished shooting. Alyssa Milano told me, ‘You really have great timing, and you could do this if you wanted to.’ And I was so taken aback by that. I was like, wow, man, someone like Alyssa Milano is telling me I could act if I really put myself into it. You know, if I really threw myself into it. So I was like, wow, for her to give me that little bit of nudge of courage, man. It was really, really awesome. That’s what I remember most about that whole thing.”

    For those curious about the episode Booker T appeared on, it was titled “Wrestling with Demons” and was the twelfth episode of the third season and the 56th overall episode of Charmed. In the episode, Booker T played a character named Thunder.
