    Which institutions did Amy Mickelson attend? | Educational Background

    When she was in her mid-teens, Amy attended Harvest Park Middle School, located at Valley Ave, Pleasanton, California.

    Following that, she was the freshman princess of Foothill high school in 1986.

    He stated that Amy’s sincere, sweet behavior overlooked her beauty.

    Battling Breast Cancer

    In 2009, Amy was diagnosed with deadly breast cancer. By the middle of 2009, she had begun her cancer treatment.

    Following her treatment, she moved to Houston, Texas, for further medication and therapy.

    In the late May of 2009, Amy’s diagnosis became public; Phil put a hold on his golf career for a moment there.

    He opted out from defending his title the following weekend.

    Mickelson wrote that there is an excellent team of doctors working on improving Amy’s condition. “We caught this early,” wrote Phil.

    Phil also wrote that the couple is waiting for the test results to guide them in the best possible direction.

    Amy had never missed any of Phil’s matches; she always stood by his side, cheering him and supporting him.

    But her battle with breast cancer stopped her from doing so for a while.

    However, Amy did show up at the 2010 Masters Tournament Finals, where Phil was playing against Lee Westwood. The event was held at the Augusta National Golf Course.

    Amy, along with her three children, showed support and encouraged Phil. Likewise, Phil Mickelson went on to snatch the trophy and won a 1.35 million dollars prize.

    In 2012, she started attending Phil’s matches after her health conditions began showing signs of improvement.

    Her health got significantly better, and she eventually recovered from breast cancer.
