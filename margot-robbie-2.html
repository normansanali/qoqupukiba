    Margot Robbie Wiki/Biography

    Born on 2 July 1990, Margot Robbie’s age is 32 Years Old as of 2022. She was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Dalby, Queensland, Australia. She is an Australian by nationality and has her belief in the Christian religion.

    Margot Robbie Childhood Pic

    Since childhood, she has had an interest in acting and modeling and has always wanted to pursue her career in the same field. As a teenager, she has worked as a bartender, house cleaner, and Subway waiter to meet her family’s financial needs.

    Career

    Margot Robbie started her career as an actress and model during high school. She has made her appearance in an independent film titled Vigilante. In the very next year, she has worked in another independent film titled I.C.U and has been featured in several television commercials. After the completion of her graduation, she moved to Melbourne to pursue her acting career more efficiently.

    In the year 2008, she made her television debut with her appearance in a guest role in the television series titled City Homicide. In the same year, she has made her appearance in the popular children’s television series titled The Elephant Princess along with popular American actor Liam Hemsworth. In the same year, she has bagged a recurring role in the television series Neighbours. After that, she moved to American and bagged her first role in the drama series titled Pan Am in the year 2011.

    In the year 2013, she has made her feature film debut with her appearance in the movie titled About Time. In the same year, she bagged a breakthrough role in a biographical black comedy titled The Wolf of Wall Street, playing the role of Jordan Belfort along with popular actor Leonardo DiCaprio.

    In the year 2014, she has started a production company named LukyChap Entertainment intending to produce more female-driven projects after the commercial success and appreciation she received for her role in the movie The Wolf of Wall Street. She has started this company along with her husband and family friends Sophia Kerr and Josey McNamara.

    In the year 2015, she has made her appearance in four back-to-back films like Focus alongside Will Smith, Suite Francaise, Z for Zachariah alongside Chris Pine, and The Big Short. In the year 2016, she has worked in the film titled Whiskey Tango Foxtrot which was a commercial failure but earned good reviews. Later in the same year, she bagged the famous role of Jane Porter in the popular adventure film titled The Legend of Tarzan.
