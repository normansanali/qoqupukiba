    New Yorker Gabby Prescod has made quite the name for herself in the fashion and media world. The new reality star is Blanc magazine's fashion director at large and has quite the impressive résumé, including working for both Bustle and Grazia over the years. We also know that Prescod isn't afraid to speak out for what's right, as she uses her social media to rally against injustice and encourage democracy. In June 2020, she shared an Instagram post encouraging her followers to get out and exercise their right to vote a few months after she posted an important message about the Black Lives Matter movement. "Today is meant to prioritize black voices. Listen to them. Make others listen to them. Let them hear us," she wrote on Blackout Tuesday.

    You might also know Prescod, though, for being one half of the Prescod Sisters, as she and her sister Danielle share much of their adventures on their joint Instagram account. Just like her sister, Danielle has an impressive career of her own, as she's the author of "Token Black Girl: A Memoir" and has hosted her own podcast, while also working for BET and InStyle.
