    Wiki/Biography

    Eugenia Sullivan Cooney was born on Wednesday, July 27, 1994 (age: 25 years, as in 2019) in Massachusetts, United States. Her zodiac sign is Leo.

    Physical Appearance

    • Height: 5′ 4″
    • Weight: 40 kg

    Family

    Eugenia Cooney with her Mother

    She has a two brother; Chip and Joshua (Half).

    She is unmarried.

    Career

    In January 2011, she opened a YouTube Channel after her name ‘Eugenia Cooney’ and started to upload videos related to fashion, which consists of outfits, make-up styles, routines, etc. She also uploads shopping videos regularly. As in October 2019, she has over 2.4 million subscribers and more than 226 million views total on her YouTube channel.

    Controversies

    • Through the second half of 2015, her viewers concerned over her health; viewers guessed that Cooney had an eating disorder presumably Anorexia Nervosa. Due to her underweight appearance, her videos began to receive more dislikes than likes.
    • Because of her skinny appearance, people requested YouTube to ban her channel. The petitioners said that Cooney suffered from an eating disorder and needed help. They also said that her extremely thin look may motivate her fans to lose a substantial amount of weight to look more like her.

    Facts

    • She was born in Massachusetts but raised in Connecticut.
    • When petitioners filed a complaint against her YouTube channel, she announced her break from Social Media in February 2019 and did not show up in the media until July 19, 2019. Later, another YouTuber, Shane Dawson uploaded a video titled ‘The Return of Eugenia Cooney’ on his YouTube channel, which features Dawson is interviewing Cooney.
    • The same day when Shane Dawson uploaded the video of Cooney’s Interview, another YouTuber and a former friend of Cooney, Jaclyn Glenn also uploaded a video on YouTube featuring herself as well as YouTubers, Evangeline DeMuro and David Michael Frank. In that video, the trio claimed that Dawson deliberately missed out a question regarding Cooney’s recovery.
    • When she took a break from social media, she met her doctor and focussed on her thin appearance.
    • Cooney is a dog lover.
