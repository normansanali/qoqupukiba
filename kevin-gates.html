    Overall Meaning

    The song "2 Phones" by Kevin Gates is about being on top and managing multiple aspects of his life. The two phones represent his dual lifestyle as a drug dealer and a ladies' man. He has one phone for his "plug," the person he gets his drugs from, and another phone for the "load," meaning the customers he sells the drugs to. He also has one phone for the women in his life and another for the money he earns. He even mentions needing two more phones because he is so busy juggling calls.


    The chorus is about Gates being so busy with his business that he is constantly receiving calls on both phones, but he is able to simultaneously count money and keep his trap jumping. The verse touches on the themes of jealousy and mistrust, as people are always questioning him about what he has and what he is willing to give. However, he remains unbothered and focused on his lucrative endeavors.


    Overall, the song showcases Gates' unapologetic nature and his ability to handle multiple responsibilities with ease.
