    Who is Cheryl Howard?

    Cheryl Howard is an American actress and writer well-known for appearing in the movies, ‘A Beautiful Mind’ and ‘Splash’. Moreover, she is also popular for writing ‘In the Face of Jinn: A Novel’.

    She is also famous as the wife of the Academy Award-winning director Ron Howard and the mother of the well-known actresses, Bryce Dallas and Paige Howard.

    Cheryl Howard: Age, Parents, Family, Siblings, Ethnicity, Childhood

    Cheryl Howard was born as Cheryl Gay Alley on December 23, 1953, in Glendale, California, USA. As of 2020, her age is 66.

    She was born to her parents, Charles Alley(father) and Vivian Schmid(mother). Her father, Charles was an amateur astronomer, chemical and aerospace engineer, and marksman.

    She also has two sisters. She was inspired by her father and was interested in traveling and adventures from her childhood days growing up watching his adventures. Her father was the first Caucasian to climb the top of Mt. Fujiyama during winter.

    Her ethnicity is Scandanavian.

    Educational Background

    Talking about her educational background, she graduated from the California State University at Northridge with a B.A. degree in Psychology.

    In addition to this, she also has obtained a Master of Fine Arts degree in screenwriting.

    Cheryl Howard: Professional Life, Career

    Acting Career

    Cheryl started her acting career with an uncredited role of a reporter in a brown hat in the debut film of her husband, ‘Grand Theft Auto which was released in 1977.

    In the 80s, she appeared in three films with her husband which were all hits at the box office. She appeared in the comedy movie, ‘Night Shift’ (1982), the fantasy film, ‘Willow’ (1988), and the fantasy romantic comedy, ‘Splash’ (1984).

    Similarly, in the 90s, she featured in two more films directed by her husband, ‘Ransom’ (1996) and ‘Edtv’ (1999).

    Furthermore, in 2001, she appeared in ‘A Beautiful Mind,’ which is a four Academy Award-winning film. The film was directed by her husband and starred Russell Crowe in the leading role.

    In the 2000s, she performed an uncredited role in ‘The Da Vinci Code (2006) and performed the role of a scientist in its sequel, ‘Angels & Demons (2009).

    Writing Career, Traveling

    She started her writing career ‘With in the Face of Jinn: A Novel’ which was published on April 1, 2005. Other books written by her include ‘Grandma, Tell Me a Story: Children’s Devotional Stories from the Farm.’ and ‘Heart & Desire’.

    Howard is also interested in traveling. She has traveled to different countries being fascinated by the diversity of different cultures and races.

    Some of the countries she has traveled to include Australia, Argentina, Mexico, Uruguay, Egypt, India, New Zealand, Afghanistan, Pakistan, and many European countries.

    In 1996-1997, she visited Central Asia which inspired her to write, ‘In the Face of Jinn: A Novel’.
