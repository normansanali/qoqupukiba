    Juice Wrld was a popular rapper, singer, and songwriter from the United States. He is best known for his 2018 released single “Lucid Dreams” which became diamond-certified after his death in December 2019. 

    The song is known to have reached no 2nd position on the US Billboard Hot 100, and was part of his debut studio album released in 2018 named “Goodbye & Good Riddance”. His second album was “Death Race for Love” released in 2019 with the hit song “Robbery”. 

    Juice Wrld Cause Of Death

    Juice died on 8th December 2019 due to a seizure attack that he suffered due to an overdose of some drugs. At the time of his death, he was flying in the private Gulfstream jet from Los Angeles to Chicago. He was fearful of the fact that he was carrying drugs on the plane, so he reportedly took multiple pills to hide them from the police while they were going through his luggage.

    He had a seizure attack after that and emergency medication was given to him. He was then taken to a nearby hospital where he was found dead. A medical examination conducted revealed that he died due to high levels of oxycodone and codeine in his body.

    According to his mother, Juice was heavily addicted to drugs from an early age. He also had anxiety disorder and suffered from depression. He even attended drug rehab some weeks before he died.

    Juice Wrld Age, Wiki

    Juice was born on 2nd December 1998 in Chicago, Illinois, United States. He had just turned 21 years old when he passed away on 8th December 2019 in Oak Lawn, Illinois, United States.

    He was brought up in the South Suburbs in Calumet Park, after which he shifted to Homewood. He completed his schooling at Homewood-Flossmoor High School in 2017. The is no record of his higher education.

    Juice Wrld Real Name

    Juice’s real name is Jarad Anthony Higgins. He was professionally known as Juice Wrld which was pronounced as Juice World and written as Juice WRLD.

    Juice Wrld Parents, Siblings

    Juice was born to Carmella Wallace (mother) and his divorced husband whose name is not known. When he was only three, his parents separated and got divorced, after which, he and his elder brother were raised singlehandedly by his mother. His father passed away in June of 2019.

    Juice Wrld Girlfriend

    Juice was in a relationship with Ally Lotti at the time of his death. They were living together in Los Angeles. They confirmed their relationship through Instagram in November 2018. After his death, she revealed in an interview that she was pregnant with his child three times but suffered miscarriages.

    Juice Wrld Net Worth
