    Sensational actress and "Claws" star, Niecy Nash, is mother to three adorable children whom she dotes on as much as she can. Meet Dom, Donnie, and Dia.

    Niecy Nash is a phenomenal actress and asides from having a successful career; she is also the mother to three adorable children — two daughters and a son.

    Amazingly, all three look like their mother, and they cherish her just as much as she loves them.

    Niecy Nash attends the 25th Annual Critics' Choice Awards on January 12, 2020. | Photo: Getty Images

    DOMINIC NASH

    Dominic is Niecy's first child, whom she had with her ex-husband, ordained minister Don Nash. The first of three children and the only male, He never fails to share how much he loves and adores the women in his life.

    During a Mother's Day celebration, he shared an adorable picture of the whole family and captioned it saying:

    "Celebrating Mother's Day with the ladies of my life, Love y'all ❤️"

    Although he had thoughts of following in his mother's footsteps as an actor, Dominic decided to pursue another career path.

    DONIELLE NASH

    Donielle is Niecy's second child with first husband Don Nash. A 25-year-old budding actress, Donielle is keen on becoming an actress and has even taken roles in some movies over the years.

    The divorce was hard on her because of the backlash she received.

    "The world is waiting on you, Babygirl. Shine bright! Be fearless! Love hard! Mama will always have your back! Go on and fly."

    Dia is also an actress like her mother and sister and has starred in various movies over the years. She even got to work closely with her mom when she played the younger version of Niecy's character.

    The Nashes are a close brood, and they never fail to gush over each other on their respective social media platforms. Each member of the family often shares images of themselves, hanging out, and having fun.

    After her divorce from her ex-husband and father of her kids Don Nash, she married Jay Tucker, although they split eight years later. The divorce was hard on her because of the backlash she received from family and fans.

    On clocking the new age, Niecy said she was glad for the way things have turned out in her life. She expressed that although she had difficulty getting to this good place, she was delighted to be in a better place.
