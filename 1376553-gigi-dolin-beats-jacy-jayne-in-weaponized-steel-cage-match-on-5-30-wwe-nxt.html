    Gigi Dolin and Jacy Jayne settled their rivalry in a brutal Weaponized Steel Cage match.

    The May 30 episode of WWE NXT kicked off with a Weaponized Steel Cage match pitting former Toxic Attraction members Gigi Dolin and Jacy Jayne against each other. Blood was shed and new bruises were formed as they fully utilized the weapons at hand including chairs, a chain, trash cans, a baseball bat wrapped in thorns, and a table.

    In the end, Dolin was able to score the victory (and redemption) by chokeslamming Jayne off the top rope and through a table.

    The win seemingly marked the end of their feud that really kickstarted in February when Jayne turned on Dolin and continued to make personal and physical attacks with each passing week.
