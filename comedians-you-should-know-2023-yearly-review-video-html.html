    18 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The new year is fast approaching, and with it will bring a new crop of “The Comedians You Should and Will Know.” But the year is not over yet, and Vulture’s 2023 honorees have some lingering thoughts on what this year has been like for them and what they’re looking forward to in the year ahead. In this video, our host, comedian and list alumnus Jay Jurden, sits down with our list of comics to watch in New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago for a series of quick chats about the nuisances of airport travel, post office bureaucracy, and social media, as well as their excitement about continuing to rack up comedy accomplishments, evening scores with their siblings, and potentially grabbing dinner with a former NBA MVP in 2024. Who better to help you grieve the passage of time?
