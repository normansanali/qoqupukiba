    Top 10 Richest Musicians In Africa

    We published an article on the Top 10 richest musicians in Nigeria and P-Square, comprising twin brothers, Peter and Paul Okoye is believed by many to be the richest Nigerian musician and was listed as number one on that list. However they are number two in the list of the Top 10 richest Musicians in Africa.

    Five Nigerians P-Square, D’banj, 2 Face, Don Jazzy and Banky W made the list and this shows the dominance of Nigerian Entertainment industry in Africa.

    Youssou N’dour, the richest musician in Africa is a Senegalese singer, percussionist, songwriter, composer, occasional actor, businessman and a politician. He is one of the most celebrated African musicians in history and is know by his track hit in 2004 “7 Seconds” .

    1. Youssou N’dour

    2. P-Square

    3. D’banj

    4. Koffi OlumideOlumide

    6. Fully Ipupa
