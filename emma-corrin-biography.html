    Emma Corrin- Bio, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

    The British actress, Emma Corrin was born on December 13, in the year 1995, in Royal Tunbridge Wells, Kent, England, the United Kingdom.

    Her birth name is Emma Louise Corrin. She is 27 years old and of British nationality as of 2023.

    Similarly, her ethnicity is mixed (English-Argentinian) and her birth sign is Sagittarius. Emma Corrin was born to Chris Corrin, her father, and Dr. Juliette Corrin, her mother. She grew up with two siblings, Richard, and Jonty.

    Talking about her education history, she went to the famous private school Woldingham in Surrey before taking a year off to study Shakespeare at LAMDA and volunteering at a school in Knysna, South Africa.

    Later, from 2015 to 2018, she studied Education with English and Theatrical at St John’s College, University of Cambridge, where they performed in multiple performances of the Cambridge University Amateur Dramatic Club, one of the UK’s oldest and most prestigious student drama clubs.

    Emma Corrin- Professional Life, Career

    Emma’s acting career began after she graduated from college. She made her television debut in 2019 as a guest star in an episode of ITV’s police drama “Grantchester.” The following season of the Epix DC American TV series Pennyworth (2019) starred her as Esme Winikus.

    The following year, she made her feature film debut as Jillian Jessup in “Misbehaviour 2020.” Philippa Lowthorpe directed the film, which she co-wrote with Keira Knightley, Gugu Mbatha-Raw, and Jessie Buckley.

    She was cast in the fourth season of Netflix’s historical drama “The Crown,” which will air in November 2020, in April 2019. Corrin was spotted playing Princess Diana. Similarly, in 2021, she was cast as Anna in The Lowry’s Harold Pinter Theatre production of “Anna X.”

    She also appeared in Orlando at London’s Garrick Theatre from November 26th, 2022 to February 25th, 2023. In the film, she marries a gay police officer, Tom (Harry Styles), who is in love with a museum curator, Patrick (David Dawson).
